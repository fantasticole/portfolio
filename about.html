Hi, I'm Cole!

I am a reliable, outgoing, detail-oriented developer who loves a challenge. I enjoy creating beautiful, engaging digital experiences using maintainable code and I absolutely LOVE squashing bugs. Function, design and bridging that gap are a few things I am passionate about.

When I'm not programming, you can find me eating, going on adventures, reading, dancing, biking around New York or revisiting Buffy the Vampire Slayer.

Background:

I started programming full time in January of this year and I haven't looked back. I was lucky enough to be able to spend 3 months at Hacker School Recurse Center, where I was surrounded by passionate programmers.

Some of my experience before diving into the tech world includes earning my B.A. in journalism from the University of North Carolina, learning how to provide great customer service, managing people and expectations, immersing myself in foreign cultures and more.

In the words of Pablo Picasso, "I am always doing that which I cannot do, in order that I may learn how to do it."
